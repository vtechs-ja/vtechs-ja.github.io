What It Is

Study together, for fun and effectiveness

Quiz Camp combines solo practice, online multiplayer, and local pass-the-phone play around a Caribbean school curriculum question bank, aligned to CSEC and primary school syllabuses. Alongside the game, a full study hub offers adaptive flashcards, a global and regional leaderboard, and an XP/badge progression system — built to maximise the quiz concept for group learning and engagement.

How It Works

Play your way, study smarter

Solo Practice

Timed quiz rounds with adaptive question selection — wrong answers surface more frequently until they stick.

Daily Challenge

One fixed challenge per day, with completion tracking and a streak calendar to keep the habit going.

Online Multiplayer

Join by a 6-character code and go head-to-head with real-time synced questions, buzzes, and live opponent scores.

Local Multiplayer

Pass-the-phone play for multiple players on one device — perfect for study groups without everyone needing their own login.

Study Hub

Flashcard-based study mode with adaptive difficulty and wrong-answer explanations shown right on the card back.

XP, Badges & Leaderboards

XP earned per game, unlockable badges, daily streaks, and country/global/city leaderboard tabs to keep the competition going.

Who It's For

Jamaican and Caribbean students

Primary Tier

Primary school students across Jamaica, practising against a curriculum-aligned question bank built for their level.

High School Tier

Secondary school students studying toward CSEC — competitive practice that makes revision feel like a game, not a chore.

Teachers & Parents

A curriculum-aligned practice tool that fits naturally alongside classroom teaching and at-home study.
