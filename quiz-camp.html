What It Is

Built around the club, not the individual

Quiz Camp has pivoted from a direct-to-student app into a coach-led club platform. The primary customer is a quiz coach or club organiser — a school club, tutoring centre, or church or community youth group — who subscribes, supplies their own question bank, and runs live-hosted Quizmaster sessions for their members. Students always join and play free through their coach's club.

Anyone else can still try the format solo through a free, ad-supported test drive — casual practice with no persistent competency tracking, since that depth only means something within a club's own curated content.

How It Works

From live hosting to daily practice

Quizmaster — Live Hosting

Coaches build a question bank with photos, video, or audio attached, then run multi-round live sessions from a phase-driven control panel — question, buzz, answer, between rounds.

Web Viewer

A no-install, browser-based display for any TV or projector — join by code, no app required for spectators watching along.

Clubs

A coach creates a club and shares an invite code. Members join free and get a club-scoped, season-labelled leaderboard that's automatically ad-free.

Club Practice & Study Hub

Curriculum-aligned practice and adaptive flashcards drawn from the coach's own question bank, with mastery tracked per member.

Free Test Drive

Solo practice and casual online or pass-the-phone multiplayer, open to anyone, ad-supported — no club or subscription required.

Club-Scoped Progression

XP, badges, streaks, and mastery tiers — the same mechanics as before, now meaningful because they're tied to a real, coach-curated question bank.

Who It's For

Coaches, their clubs, and casual players

Quiz Coaches & Club Organisers

School quiz clubs, tutoring centres, and church or community youth groups who want a turnkey live-quiz and practice tool for their own students.

Club Members

Students who join free through their coach, with full access to the club's content, tracked competency, and the club leaderboard.

Casual Players

Anyone who wants to try the format solo — ad-supported, no club or subscription required.

For Coaches

Every plan includes the full question bank, competency-tracked study centre, daily challenge, club leaderboards, live Quizmaster hosting, and automatic ad-free access for every member.
