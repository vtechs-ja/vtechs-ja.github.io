About Vortex

A Product Company
at Our Core

Vortex Technologies Limited is a Jamaican technology company with a singular focus: building software products that solve real problems. From ideation through design, engineering, and launch, we take ideas and turn them into polished, production-ready products.

Beyond our own product work, we partner with businesses to deliver bespoke software solutions, manage cloud infrastructure, and provide strategic technology consultancy — all grounded in the same engineering discipline that drives our internal products.
