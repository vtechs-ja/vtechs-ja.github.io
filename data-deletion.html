Privacy Rights

Data Deletion Request

Submit a request to permanently delete your account and personal data from any Vortex Technologies product.

What Happens After You Submit

1

We receive your request

Your request is sent directly to our privacy team. We aim to acknowledge all requests within 2 business days.

2

We verify your identity

To protect account holders, we will confirm your identity before processing any deletion. We'll contact you at the email address associated with your account.

3

We process the deletion

Once verified, we delete your account and associated personal data. We aim to complete all deletions within 30 days of verification, in line with applicable data protection law.

4

We confirm completion

You'll receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete. Access to any associated accounts is permanently revoked at this point.

What Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification the following will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Personal information provided during registration
  • Usage data and preferences tied to your account
  • Any content or data you created within the product

This action is irreversible. Once your data has been deleted it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have saved anything you wish to keep before submitting.

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained after your request is processed, where required by law or legitimate business necessity:

  • Financial and billing records (required for tax and legal compliance)
  • A record of your deletion request itself (for audit and compliance purposes)
  • Anonymised or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you
  • Any data we are legally obligated to retain under applicable law

Retained data is held for the minimum period required and is not used for marketing or product improvement.

Household-linked products (Common Ground, HomeBase): Deleting your account removes your personal profile and login credentials. Data entered into a shared household — financial records in Common Ground, or task and check-in history in HomeBase — may remain visible to other active household members. To request full household data removal, all household members should submit requests, or the household admin can contact us directly. For HomeBase child members (profile-only accounts with no email), deletion requests should be submitted by the parent or household admin.

Need Help or Have Questions?

If you have questions about this process or want to discuss your options before submitting a request, you can reach our team directly at info@vtechs.com.jm.

You can also review our Privacy Policy for full details on how we collect, use, and protect your data.
